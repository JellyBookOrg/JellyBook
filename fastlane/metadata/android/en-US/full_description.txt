<p><i>JellyBook</i> is a book and comic reading app for Jellyfin. Its a app thats meant to allow you to read your files from Jellyfin on mobile. Supported file formats include:</p><ul><li><code>.cbr</code> &amp; <code>.rar</code>: cbr (Comic Book aRchive) is a proprietary file format so is not necessarily encouraged.</li><li><code>.cbz</code> &amp; <code>.zip</code>: another Comic book format</li><li><code>.pdf</code>: the Portable Document Format as you know it</li><li><code>.epub</code>: Electronic PUBlication is a wide-spread eBook format</li></ul><p>More formats are to be added over time.</p>
